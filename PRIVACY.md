# Privacy Policy

This repository ships a public-facing Gradio Space for exploring bounded, fixture-backed
artifacts from the `nlp-policy-nz` pipeline.

## Data Collected and Displayed

- Parliamentary and legislative text may include MP names, electorate references, speaker names, and quoted content.
- Fixture-mode pages load checked-in artefacts only. Uploaded files are processed locally in the running Space session.
- The Space does not intentionally collect user accounts, passwords, or persistent analytics data.

## Retention

- Checked-in repository artifacts are retained in git history until explicitly removed.
- Uploaded files, generated tables, and browser-session state are ephemeral and should be deleted after use.
- Deployed Space infrastructure may retain standard platform logs according to the hosting provider's policies.

## Deletion and Correction Requests

If you want a document or record deleted or corrected, email `privacy@nlp-policy-nz.example` and include:

- the dataset source or upload context
- the document identifier or URL
- the requested deletion or correction

Requests that affect repository history, published releases, or third-party mirrors may require manual follow-up.

## Third-Party Processors

- Hugging Face for Space hosting and deployment
- Zenodo for release archiving
- OSF for release archiving

Use of those services is subject to their published terms and privacy policies.

## Accessibility and Compliance

This Space is intended to meet WCAG 2.1 AA / NZ Web Accessibility Standard expectations by default,
including keyboard navigation, visible focus, and descriptive labels.
