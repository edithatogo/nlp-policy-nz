(1) This Act is the Legislation (Repeals and Amendments) Act 2024.
(2) In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,—
    (a) commencement means the date on which this Act receives the Royal assent; and
    (b) enactment means an enactment (within the meaning of section 29 of the Legislation Act 2019) that is repealed or amended by Schedule 1.
(3) Section 5 of the Māori Language Act 2016 provides that Te Reo Māori is an official language of New Zealand.
(4) The Minister must, no later than 1 July 2025, prepare a report on the operation of tikanga Māori within the scope of this enactment.
(5) Despite subsection (4), the provisions of this Act do not apply to any taonga found on land to which section 186 of the Resource Management Act 1991 applies.
