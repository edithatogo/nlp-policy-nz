# FOI-O Archive Extraction Adapter Evidence

## Implemented milestone

The versioned adapter, deterministic candidate bundle, baseline comparison,
provenance preservation, bounded evaluation fixture, and focused tests are
implemented. The milestone is a contract pilot, not production legal-label
validation.

## Independent review panel — 2026-07-15

Three independent reviews considered implementation quality, programme
governance, and legal/evidentiary promotion safety. They agreed that the
adapter correctly preserves the candidate-only boundary, but the fixture uses
placeholder immutable-looking revisions and synthetic sources. The available
permission-family performance and calibration evidence also do not justify a
production promotion claim.

## Promotion decision

**No promotion approved.** All NZ/archive labels remain candidates. The review
is complete because it reached a fail-closed decision; it is not represented
as human legal validation. Real source/profile/model pins, rights-reviewed
held-out samples, independent annotation, adjudication, and per-label metrics
are tracked by Track 93 and issue #129.
