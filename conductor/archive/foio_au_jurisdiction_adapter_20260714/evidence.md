# FOI-O Australian Jurisdiction Adapter Evidence

## Implemented milestone

The Commonwealth/NSW routing, abstention, cross-profile contamination guard,
candidate bundle, provenance, metrics contract, and focused tests are
implemented. No other Australian jurisdiction is enabled by this milestone.

## Independent review panel — 2026-07-15

Three independent reviews considered implementation quality, programme
governance, and legal/evidentiary promotion safety. They agreed that profile
isolation and fail-closed routing are sound contract mechanisms, but the pilot
uses synthetic `example.test` sources and placeholder immutable-looking pins.
Those fixtures cannot establish Commonwealth or NSW legal-label validity.

## Promotion decision

**No promotion approved for Commonwealth or NSW.** Outputs remain candidates,
and expansion to the other states and territories remains disabled. Real
legislation/source pins, rights-reviewed jurisdiction-isolated held-out data,
independent annotation and adjudication, and separate jurisdiction metrics are
tracked by Track 93 and issue #129. No human legal approval is claimed.
