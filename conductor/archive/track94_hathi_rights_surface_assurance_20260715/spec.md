# Track 94: HathiTrust-NZ Rights and Surface Assurance

## Goal

Close the gap between the implemented Track 86 ingestion contract and authoritative rights provenance, executable advertised interfaces, and adversarial compatibility assurance.

## Requirements

- Record authoritative rights URI/snapshot hash, access time, territorial applicability, reviewer, and policy version.
- Separate acquisition, processing, full-text publication, and derived-feature permissions.
- Implement and test, or remove, each advertised CLI/API/SDK/MCP surface.
- Add property, mutation, migration, schema-compatibility, and malformed-rights tests.

## Acceptance

Every public-full-text work item has affirmative reviewable rights evidence and every advertised interface is executable and tested.
