**Dependencies**: Tracks 34-36

**Status**: Planned

## Goal

Create a submission-ready manuscript package for arXiv, including manuscript skeleton, abstract, figures, tables, supplementary documents, standards checklist, and review-agent loops.

## Scope

- Manuscript, abstract, figure, table, and supplement skeletons.
- Peer, editor, expert, standards, reproducibility, and arXiv compliance reviewer agents.
- 100-point rubrics and pass threshold above 95/100 for every aspect and overall.
- Evidence-bounded review loop records, scores, fixes, and unresolved blockers.

- [ ] Create manuscript, abstract, figures, tables, and supplementary skeletons.
