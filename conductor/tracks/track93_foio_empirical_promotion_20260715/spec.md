# Track 93: FOI-O Empirical Promotion Evidence

## Goal

Replace synthetic placeholder evidence with immutable, rights-reviewed and jurisdiction-isolated empirical evidence for NZ/archive, Australian Commonwealth, and NSW FOI-O candidates.

## Requirements

- Pin real FOI-O, fyi-archive, legislation/profile, model, and sample revisions.
- Maintain separate NZ/archive, Commonwealth, and NSW held-out evaluations.
- Record reviewer/adjudicator provenance and disagreement decisions.
- Report per-family and per-jurisdiction precision, recall, F1, calibration, coverage, and abstention.
- Fail closed: no jurisdiction or label family is promoted from another jurisdiction's evidence.

## Acceptance

Each jurisdiction has a reproducible promotion or no-promotion decision based on real immutable evidence; placeholder pins and synthetic sources are rejected.
