# Ontology guide

The ontology layer turns extracted text features into durable legal and
parliamentary concepts.

- Deontic extraction captures obligations, permissions, prohibitions, and legal
  effect cues.
- Temporal extraction captures dates, commencement, deadlines, and validity
  windows.
- Entity extraction links people, organizations, Acts, Bills, and provisions.
- Argument and stance extraction supports Hansard debate analysis.
- The ontology mapping graph records explicit crosswalks between standards such
  as LKIF, Akoma Ntoso, ODRL, DCAT, NZGLS, SKOS, FOAF, SIOC, and PROV-O.

Rules-as-code is one output family of the broader extraction framework, not the
only purpose of the repo.
